Our Story

Trust & Excellence

Founded in 2009, BrightSmile began with a single chair and a simple promise — that every patient deserves exceptional dental care delivered with genuine compassion.

Who We Are

Born from a Passion
for Better Dental Care

BrightSmile Dental Clinic was founded by Dr. Arun Sharma in 2009 with a vision to break the stereotype that going to the dentist has to be an unpleasant experience. What started as a two-room practice in Deccan Gymkhana has grown into one of Pune's most trusted multi-specialty dental centres.

Today, our team of 12 specialist dentists, 8 hygienists, and 20+ support staff serve over 4,500 patients across Pune. We've invested heavily in the latest technology, the warmest interiors, and the most compassionate team — so that every patient leaves smiling, and not just because of the treatment.
