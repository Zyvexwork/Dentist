Quick Answers

Common Questions

Can't find what you need? Send us a message or call — we're always happy to help.

Book an Appointment
How quickly do you respond to messages?
During clinic hours (Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM) we typically reply within 47 minutes on average. WhatsApp messages are usually answered fastest. Outside hours, we respond first thing the next morning.
Can I request a callback instead of calling?
Absolutely. Use the contact form above and select "General Enquiry." Mention your preferred callback time in the message and our front desk will call you back at your convenience.
Do you offer teleconsultations?
Yes! We offer 15-minute video consultations for patients who want a quick opinion, a treatment plan review, or are located far from the clinic. Book through the appointment page and select "Tele-Consultation" as the service.
How do I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Call us or WhatsApp at least 24 hours before your appointment and we will reschedule at no charge. Last-minute cancellations (under 4 hours) may incur a small administrative fee for specialist appointments.
Is there a patient portal where I can view my records?
We are currently building our patient portal, launching in Q3 2025. In the meantime, you can request your treatment records, X-rays, and invoices by emailing records@brightsmile.in and we will send them within 24 hours.
